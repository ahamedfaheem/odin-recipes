Fresh Homemade Pasta

Homemade pasta is soft, fresh, and full of flavor. With just flour, eggs, and a little effort, you can make pasta that tastes much better than store-bought. It’s simple, fun to prepare, and perfect for any sauce you love.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour, eggs, salt, and olive oil to make dough
  2. Knead until smooth, then let it rest for 30 minutes
  3. Roll out the dough thin
  4. Cut into pasta shapes
  5. Boil in salted water for 2–4 minutes

Fresh Homemade Spaghetti

Homemade spaghetti is simple and delicious. With just a few ingredients, you can make a warm and comforting meal. It’s quick, easy, and perfect for any day

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil or cheese

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water for 8–10 minutes
    2. Heat olive oil, add garlic, and cook until golden
  2. Add tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer 5 minutes
  3. Drain spaghetti, mix with the sauce
  4. Serve hot with basil or cheese

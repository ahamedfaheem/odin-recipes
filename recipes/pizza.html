Fresh Homemade Pizza

Fresh homemade pizza is warm, cheesy, and full of flavor. With a simple dough, tasty sauce, and your favorite toppings, you can make a meal that’s better than takeout. It’s fun to prepare, quick to bake, and always satisfying to share

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables, herbs, or chicken pepperoni

Steps

  1. Mix flour, yeast, water, olive oil, and salt to make a soft dough
  2. Let the dough rise until it doubles in size
  3. Roll it out into a pizza base
  4. Spread tomato sauce on top
  5. Add cheese and toppings
  6. Bake in a hot oven until the crust is golden and the cheese melts
